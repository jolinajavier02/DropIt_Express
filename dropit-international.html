Prohibited Items in Aviation

Critical safety regulations for air-freight shipments

Liquid, Powder, Pastes, Granular Product

Receiving operation requirements:
- Liquids, powders, pastes, and granular objects can be euphemistically refused and not accepted if the safety attributes of the consignment cannot be determined;
- In the case that the consignment is determined to be non-illegal or non-dangerous goods (the customer is required to provide safety instructions), it can be arranged for transportation by truck.

Battery Operated Products

- Electromechanical separation, battery independent, insulated, shockproof packaging; make sure that the battery is detached before shipment.
- The all-in-one machine is forbidden to collect;
- It is forbidden to accept and send batteries, and lithium batteries with a capacity of more than 20,000 mAh (100Wh) are prohibited from receiving and sending.

Explosive Materials

TNT, gunpowder, firecrackers, and any pressure-sensitive explosive devices are strictly banned from all airline transport.

Toxic & Infections

Biological hazards, viruses, infectious bacteria, and chemical poisons that pose a massive risk to airline personnel and passengers.

Restricted Items

Items requiring airline clearance or specific international documentation

Pharmaceuticals

Requires medical prescription and international safety data sheets (MSDS).

High-Value Goods

Jewelry, precious stones, and rare metals require specific insurance and secure transport permits.

Sensitive Documents

Passports, legal deeds, and original certifications require tracked high-priority handling.

International Shipping Terms

Legal guidelines for global transport and aviation safety

Aviation Safety Compliance

All shipments must comply with IATA (International Air Transport Association) regulations. Any item found to endanger the aircraft or its passengers will be seized by airport security.

Customs & Duties

The sender or receiver is responsible for any import duties, taxes, or customs fees levied by the destination country. DropIt International handles clearance but cannot waive legal fees.

Global Weight Limits

Max weight per individual parcel via air freight is 30kg for standard international express. Heavier items require palletized freight services and 48-hour advance notice.

Delivery Lead Times

Estimates are subject to flight schedules, airline availability, and local customs processing times at the destination port.
