Terms and Conditions

Standard Rates

Standard rates apply with an additional premium service fee for Door-to-Door, Walk-in, and VIP transactions. Other discounts and promotions are not applicable to this service.

Accepted Items

Only parcels availing of the J&T Super delivery are acceptable. Items prohibited in aviation will be rejected.

Super Service

The J&T Super (SP) service is applicable for Walk-in, Door-to-Door (D2D) Free Pickup Service, and VIP Clients.

Air Transport

Customers shipping from NCR/Luzon to Visayas or Mindanao with packages limited to:

  • 5kg only
  • Contain non-prohibited items suitable for air transport

Nationwide Coverage

The service is available nationwide. Shipments from the National Capital Region (NCR) and Greater Manila Area (GMA) are transported via air to Visayas and Mindanao, while all other routes are delivered on land with improved lead times guaranteed.
